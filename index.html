Our Story

Meet Chef Syed

Introducing Chef Syed — our master Artisan Head Pastry Chef whose culinary artistry redefines luxury desserts. With an exceptional track record in Michelin-starred establishments worldwide, including prestigious venues such as Hilton Hotels, St Martins Lane, Bingham, Do & Co, Lensbury, and Barnsdale, Chef Syed brings unparalleled expertise to every creation.

Specializing in crafting exquisite wedding cakes, gourmet French macarons, and stunning plated desserts, he elevates each piece to a work of art. His innovative approach also caters to dietary needs, offering egg-free, vegan, and allergen-friendly options without compromising on flavour or sophistication.

Whether it's an intimate celebration or a grand event, Chef Syed's visionary desserts promise to delight, impress, and leave a lasting impression. Experience the pinnacle of pastry mastery with a Chef whose passion and precision turn dessert into an unforgettable experience.

Get in Touch

Ready to Order?

We take orders for weddings, celebrations, and special events. All creations are made to order, ensuring the freshest and most personal experience. Get in touch early to secure your date — our calendar fills quickly.

We recommend that inquiries be made at least 3 to 4 weeks prior to ordering a wedding cake, 1 to 2 weeks for standard cakes, and 3 to 4 working days for macaron orders. Delivery services are available, with charges based on the delivery distance.
